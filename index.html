My Story

Hello, there! I'm Ana, a junior software developer enthusiast who loves cracking code puzzles and playing hide-and-seek in the code. You could say I'm a bit of a code detective!

During my previous role as a sales and marketing analyst, I discovered a passion for the technical aspects, specifically in debugging errors and comprehending code while utilizing macros with the developer tab option.

While on maternity leave, I seized the opportunity to embark on a transformative 16-week rigorous boot camp designed to facilitate the transition of individuals from diverse backgrounds into the tech field. It was like a rollercoaster ride, thrilling and intense and I successfully completed the program, equipping myself with valuable skills and knowledge.

Now, I'm excited to contribute my analytical and problem-solving skills to a mission-driven team.
